Bussard Family Meatloaf

Unveiling our cherished family meatloaf recipe! Perfect for those nights when we want to indulge in a wholesome, delicious meal without breaking the bank. Crafted with love and tradition, this meatloaf is a hearty blend of flavors that brings us all together. It's our go-to for a satisfying, nutritious dinner that makes healthy eating feel like a treat. Join us in the kitchen and discover the magic of this beloved family classic!

Items Required

  • 2 lbs of 93% lean meat of your choice
  • 1 cup of seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of celery
  • 1/2 cup of green onion
  • Onion powder to taste
  • Garlic powder with parsley to taste
  • Basil to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Basil to taste
  • Paprikia to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Mix all your ingredients in a bowl
  3. Put them in a baking dish
  4. Bake in oven for 1 hour, or until internal temp is safe
